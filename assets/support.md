# welcome to scratch

scratch is a tiny editor for `.txt` files. open one, edit it, save it back. nothing fancy. that's the point.

next week, we're plugging in AI for summaries and quizzes. for now, just the basics.

---

## getting started

just open the editor and start typing. your work auto-saves to your browser as you go, so closing the tab won't kill it.

to open an existing file, hit **open** in the toolbar.

to save your work to your computer, hit **save .txt**.

## what the buttons do

- **new** — toss the current scratch and start fresh
- **open** — load any `.txt` or `.md` file from your computer
- **save .txt** — download your work as a text file
- **A− / A+** — make the text smaller or bigger
- **summarize this** — coming next week, will hand the AI your text and get a summary back
- **make a quiz** — coming next week, will turn your text into a multiple-choice quiz

## faq

**is this free?**
yeah. completely. AI features will use Groq's free tier when we add them next week.

**where's my stuff stored?**
in your browser, on your device. nothing leaves your computer (except eventually the AI requests, which go straight to Groq).

**does it work on phone?**
yep, the layout adapts to small screens.

**what if i clear my browser data?**
everything's gone. so save important stuff to your computer with **save .txt** if it matters.

## keyboard shortcuts

- `Ctrl + S` save the file
- `Ctrl + O` open a file
- `Tab` insert two spaces
